## 2026: Scale Decision

**Path A: Working (~$5k+ MRR by Dec 2025)**
- Consider going full-time
- Hire first employee(s)
- Raise small pre-seed or bootstrap
- Expand to adjacent niches

**Path B: Not Working (<$2k MRR)**
- Honest assessment: continue or kill?
- Pivot positioning/niche
- Consider acquisition
- Move on to next thing

## Reality Check

**Part-time constraints:**
- ~10-15 hours/week available
- Scope must stay tight
- Automate or delegate early
- Say "no" to most features

**If falling behind:**
- Push timeline, don't cut quality
- Be honest about what's realistic
- Better to delay than launch half-baked
