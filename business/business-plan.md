# Galeria Business Plan

> Event photo sharing + attendance tracking platform
> Last updated: 2025-03-04

---

## Executive Summary

**Galeria** is an event management platform that helps organizers collect photos, track attendance, and run engagement features like lucky draws—all in one place.

**Problem:** Event organizers struggle with fragmented tools for photos, check-ins, and guest engagement.

**Solution:** Unified platform with QR code check-ins, shared photo galleries, and built-in engagement features.

**Business Model:** B2B SaaS with tiered subscriptions (Free, Pro, Premium, Enterprise)

---

## Value Proposition

### For Event Organizers

| Pain | Solution |
|------|----------|
| Guests photos scattered across phones/cameras | Shared gallery where everyone uploads |
| Manual check-in lists and paper sign-ins | QR code scanning, instant verification |
| Boring events with low engagement | Lucky draws, photo challenges, reactions |
| Multiple tools, multiple costs | All-in-one platform |

### Primary Use Cases

- **Weddings:** Guest photo collection + seating check-in
- **Corporate events:** Attendance tracking + shared gallery
- **Birthday parties:** Casual photo sharing + fun engagement
- **Conferences:** Professional check-in + networking gallery

## Revenue Model

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | Target | Key Limits |
|------|-------|--------|------------|
| Free | $0 | Try-before-buy | 1 event/month, 20 photos |
| Pro | $29/mo | Regular organizers | 10 events, 100 photos, lucky draw |
| Premium | $79/mo | Agencies, power users | 50 events, 500 photos, video |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large organizations | Unlimited everything |

### Revenue Assumptions (Post-Launch)

| Month | Goal | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1-3 | 10 paying customers | Validation |
| 4-6 | 50 paying customers | ~$1,000-1,500 MRR |
| 7-12 | 200 paying customers | ~$4,000-6,000 MRR |

## Competitive Positioning

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Edge |
|------------|-----------|------------|----------|
| PicEvent | Established brand | Expensive, clunky | Modern UX, better price |
| Pixxo | Good mobile app | Limited features | All-in-one platform |
| WeddingWire | Huge audience | Events marketplace, not tool | Focused on photos + engagement |

### Indirect Competitors

- Google Photos (shared albums)
- Dropbox (file sharing)
- Eventbrite (ticketing, not photos)
- Instagram (not event-specific)

**Our moat:** Integrated attendance + photos + engagement in one simple experience

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Low conversion from free to paid | High | Generous free trial, clear upgrade triggers |
| Can't compete with established players | High | Focus on underserved niche first |
| Churn after single event | Medium | Annual plans, event templates, retention features |
| Part-time constrains growth | Medium | Automate operations, keep scope tight |
